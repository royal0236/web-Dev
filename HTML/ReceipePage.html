Vegetable Fried Rice

A quick and tasty homemade fried rice recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup chopped vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add garlic and onion, sauté until light brown.
  3. Add vegetables and cook for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  5. Add soy sauce and salt.
  6. Cook for 2 minutes and serve hot.
